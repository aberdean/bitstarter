FAQ

How does it work?
Joining textMOOCs gives you the possibility to share your notes and help create new textMOOCs for students to buy.
What's in it for me if I help creating textMOOCs?
You will be having an active role in changing the world of education forever! Literally millions of people will be able to take advantage of your work. Plus, 10% of all profits from the sale of each textMOOC will be split among the students who created it.
What if I cannot afford buying a textMOOC?
We have a special scholarship fund for students who cannot afford to buy textMOOCs, so you will be able to apply for a scholarship and get the textMOOCs you need free of charge.
